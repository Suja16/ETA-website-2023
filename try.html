Our Vision

TO BUILD A FUEL EFFICIENT VEHICLE TO COMBAT CURRENT PROBLEMS AND CREATE A SUSTAINABLE FUTURE FOR COMING GENERATIONS.

We are Eta, a compassionate group of young engineers who believe in bringing a change to the traditional conventions followed in today's automobile industry leading to a carbon neutral Earth. In manufacturing each of our vehicles, at macroscopic levels, we have reduced the carbon footprint since we have been established.Our main goal this season is to incorporate the learnings from the previous seasons' events and convert our regional victory into a global victory.

Designed by

MAHIL

2019

The Hardworks of Team to give shape to ideas to achieve the best work ever, paid of in 2019. Justifying the Name given, Mahil succeded in securing the First Rank in Shell Eco-Marathon, India 2019. By knocking an unbelievable mileage of 270 KmpL. Mahil was the first prototype to complete the technical inspections at the event amongst 23 other teams, Winning the Safety Award, setting a benchmark and motivation for Team.

Triton

2017

Keeping the legacy of it's previous prototype intact, Team ETA stood up to it's name with their latest and most advanced prototype Triton. Engineered to perfection, this prototype was the first one from India to participate in Shell Eco Marathon Asia 2017, Singapore having a carbon fibre monocoque structured body.

ARYA

2015

Participated in Shell Eco Marathon Asia 2015, Philippines. This first prototype of Team ETA to have a carbon fibre shell to improve aerodynamics along with a aluminium spaceframe for strength and rigidity. This prototype not only stood 5th in Asia, but also helped to officially mark ourselves the best team in India with an astonishing mileage of 153 kmpl.

J-14

2014

The successor of the '13 prototype J-14 with not just an improved design but with a glass fibre body shell along with the saffron -white - green aesthetic design representing the Indian tricolour was the highlight of the prototype.

JUGAAD

2013

Jugaad, was the first prototype built by Team ETA. Participated in Shell Eco Marathon Asia 2013, Malaysia this sheet metal prototype was designed with sheer determination and innovation and hence named, Jugaad.
